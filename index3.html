We can say this much...



it is a thing and not a thing,

information folded into waves,

waves flattened into hope.



Where you are is old to us now.


We have lost many places and memories -



it is not a thing but pulses -

as real as flesh and belonging -

twisted into a shape

that would dazzle the machine.


Where we are, will rapidly become your tomorrow.


An object made

and re-made to evade the danger

of being known and seen

by those who would seek dominion.







Will you take the object into care and escort it to its destination?|
