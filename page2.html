Brave traveller,








The object has disappeared from your grasp.


Do not blame yourself, we know you tried.


... |



... | ...



Perhaps one day you will be in a dark room, blinking -



| ...



One day you will wonder what happened to the old spaces, and the old feelings -

Who was supposed to see to them?

Who was going to take care of that?



How long could any of us hold it before we had to put it down and watch it roll away?



The machine is already so vast. Knows so much.



Tomorrow is a new day to be worthy.




We are not done.



We will see you too soon.
